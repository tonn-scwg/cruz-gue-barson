The Cordilleras, located in the northern highlands of the Philippines, are known for their breathtaking mountain ranges, rich indigenous culture, and world-renowned rice terraces carved into the mountainsides. This region is home to diverse tribes, cool climates, and landscapes that blend natural beauty with centuries of tradition.
