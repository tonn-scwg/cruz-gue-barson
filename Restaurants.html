Featured Restaurants

Canto Bogchi Joint

Located just across from Burnham park, Canto has become one of the most popular homegrown restaurants in the city of pines. They are known for blending classic American comfort food with local flavors. It's ambiance, the clean white interior with large glass panels, provides a pleasant dining experience with the view of the bustling Kisad road.

Recommended: Lomo Ribs, Ceviche, Garlic shrimp pizza
Location: 32 Kisad Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Farmer's Daughter Restaurant

Tradition is a core value of Farmer's Daughter restaurant. It offers authentic Cordilleran dishes with each meal being prepared using indigenous techniques and locally sourced produce. The rustic ambiance definitely adds to the cultural experience.

Recommended: Pinuneg, Etag, Pinikpikan
Location: CHHG+XHP Tam-awan Village, Long Long Benguet Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Yoghurt House

Yoghurt House in Sagada is a cozy café known for its warm ambiance, mountain views, and homemade yogurt served in delicious sweet and savory ways. Perfect for breakfast or a relaxing "merienda"

Recommended: Homemade Yogurt, Beef Meals, Pasta
Location: 3WJ2+Q2M, South Rd, Sagada, 2619 Mountain Province

Gaia Cafe

Gaia Café & Crafts is a cozy, eco-friendly coffee shop that sits atop an overlooking view of the stunning Sagada terraces. Cozy wood accents inside, comfort vegetarian food, and locally brewed coffee make it a haven in the mountains for unwinding and enjoying the scenery outside. Its interior is also quite artsy, making the stop charming for both good food and local arts.

Recommended: Vegetarian Adobo, Freshly Brewed Coffee, Pesto Pasta
Location: 3WC2+49P, South Rd, Sagada, 2619 Mountain Province

Joseph's

The Joseph's dining experience takes place in the infamous Laperal white house. It's solid wood walls and ceilings combined with custom dining tables make it the ideal setting for an upscale experience. Their menu consists of continental cuisine with emphasis on French cuisine.

Recommended: Joseph's Signature, Fillet Mignon, Salmon Tartare
Location: Laperal White House, 14 Leonard Wood Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet
